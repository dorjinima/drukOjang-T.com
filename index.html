Journey to explore Bhutan

Bhutan, a Himalayan kingdom, is known for its stunning landscapes, ancient monasteries, and rich cultural heritage. It values Gross National Happiness over GDP, blending tradition with modernity. The country's unique architecture, vibrant festivals, and pristine environment make it a peaceful and spiritual destination, deeply rooted in Buddhism.
